codex-buddy invite helper

A local, readable helper for givers who need to move a matched seeker email into the official OpenAI Codex referral flow.

Official flow only. This page does not send invites, does not call OpenAI endpoints, does not read cookies or tokens, and does not make network requests.

Manual flow

  1. Open the official Codex or ChatGPT surface where your account normally shows referral or invite controls.
  2. Paste the seeker email there. Review the official UI before sending.
  3. Send the invite only if the official UI says your account is eligible and the action is available.
  4. Return to Telegram and tap Invite sent only after the official UI accepts the action.

If your current device cannot show the official referral UI, use another trusted device or browser session where the official UI is available.

Audit notes

  • No external scripts, styles, images, or libraries.
  • No automatic network activity and no forms.
  • No cookies, local storage, tokens, or account data access.
  • No OpenAI invite endpoint calls.
  • Email exists only in this browser tab until you close or clear it.
